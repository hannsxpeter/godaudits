# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Everyone participating in the godaudits project (issues, pull requests,
discussions) is expected to:

- Be direct about the work and decent to the people. Critique plans, prose,
  and prompts as hard as you like; do not attack the person who wrote them.
- Assume competence and good faith, especially across language barriers.
- Keep discussions on the work. No harassment, no discrimination, no
  sustained derailing, no doxxing, no sexualized content.
- Respect maintainer decisions on scope. Disagreement is welcome once,
  reargued forever is not.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, locked threads, or bans from the
repository at the maintainer's discretion. Report conduct issues privately
to the maintainer via GitHub rather than in public threads.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is a condensed statement in the spirit of the
Contributor Covenant (contributor-covenant.org), adapted for a small
single-maintainer repository.
